Naša misija

Our mission

Viva La Musica pokušava da spoji narod sa klasičnom muzikom i njenim kompozitorima, a takođe i da pokuša da omogućilakše upoznavanje sa temama kroz koje su slavni kompozitori stvarali svoja dela koja se smatraju blagom.

Viva La Musica tries to connect the people with classical music and its composers, and also to try to enable an easier introduction to the themes through which famous composers created their works that are considered treasures.

Ne znate note? Nije problem

Don't know music notes? No problem

Ako niste upoznati, ili ste zaboravili note, smatramo da vam to ne sme praviti problem, zato smo pripremili stranicu gde se možete upoznati sa i obnoviti muzičke note kada god to želite.

If you are not familiar, or have forgotten the notes, we consider that shouldn't be a problem for you, that's why we've prepared a page where you can familiarize yourself with and renew musical notes whenever you want.

Pogledajte slavne kompozitore

See the famous composers

Dela ovih kompozitora se uče u školama i već stotinama godina se smatraju nacionalnim i kulturnim blagom. Iako ste se verovatno već upoznati sa dobrim delom značajnih kompozitora, uvek je dobro vratiti se i pogledati par puta na ono kroz šta ste se već upoznali, a možete i videti one sa kojima se do sada niste sreli.

The works of these composers are taught in schools and have been considered for hundreds of years national and cultural treasure. Although you are probably already familiar with a good part important composers, it's always good to go back and look a couple of times at what you've been through already met, and you can also see those you haven't met before.
